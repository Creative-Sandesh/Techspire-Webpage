About Techspire College

Best Management & IT College in Kathmandu, Nepal

Techspire, located in New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, provides modern facilities, emphasizing a conducive learning environment. In 2017, the college partnered with the Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU), offering world-class tech and business education in Nepal. This collaboration ensures international exposure and prepares students for dynamic and competitive industries in IT and business. Techspire College facilitates tech learning through partnership with Code Himalaya, one of Nepal's rapidly expanding IT enterprises.

Industry Embedded Curriculum

Three Key Pillars in developing industry embedded curriculum

Learning Pedagogy

Academic classes for four days a week, where they receive theoretical instruction and engage in discussions related to the curriculum. Additionally, there are two days dedicated to practical classes, allowing students to apply their knowledge in hands-on learning experience.

Non credit course for practical exposure

To further enhance the learning process, there is an extra one-hour class every day, providing additional support and reinforcing key concepts. This innovative approach aims to strike a balance between theoretical understanding and real-world application, promoting a more holistic and effective learning experience for students.

Leadership Development

Through leadership development initiatives, students can hone their leadership qualities, communication skills, and decision-making abilities, preparing them for future leadership roles in their careers. Furthermore, capacity building activities focus on enhancing students' capabilities and competencies, equipping them with the necessary skills to excel in their chosen fields.
